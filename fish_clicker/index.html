Simple Fish Clicker

How to play: click the fish to attack it. Use gold gained to purchase health and buffs. Don't let your health go to 0 or you lose! Get the most gold possible.

Each round will utilize different algorithms to determine damage and gold. At the end, you can compare all the round results.
